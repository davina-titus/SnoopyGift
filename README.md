# 🐾 A Little Something For You

> *"For the one who makes every night feel like stargazing"*

A handcrafted, interactive love gift built with pure HTML, CSS & vanilla JavaScript. No frameworks. No dependencies. Just something made with care.

**🌐 Live:** https://teal-dieffenbachia-83c6e9.netlify.app/

---

## ✨ Experience

The gift unfolds in four scenes as you scroll:

**✉️ The Envelope**
A floating envelope in a starry night sky. Tap it to open everything.

**🌙 Snoopy Stargazing**
Snoopy lies on his doghouse under the moon with Woodstock by his side — hand-drawn in SVG, gently floating.

**📖 A Scrapbook of You**
Tape-pinned cards capturing little moments — the sleepy-happy look, the real laugh, the feeling of safety, songs that are too many to count.

**🕺 Snoopy's Dance**
Snoopy and Woodstock dance under a disco light, just because you exist. Hit *Send Love Back* to burst hearts across the screen.

**💕 The Final Message**
*"Not just on special days. Not just when things are easy. Every ordinary Tuesday."*

---

## 🛠 Built With

- HTML5 & CSS3 — animations, SVG art, scroll reveals
- Vanilla JavaScript — no libraries
- Google Fonts — Playfair Display, Caveat, Nunito
- Deployed on Netlify

## 🚀 Run Locally

```bash
# No install needed — just open in browser
open index.html
```
